Instructions

During this task,you will see black dots appear randomly on the screen. Your goal is to click on as many of these black dots as possible while also counting backward by 3 (series 1), then 7 (series 2), then 17 (series 3) from the numbers provided by the study coordinator. Please make sure to maintain counting as you click on the dots. Each series will last for 4 minutes. You will have a 90 second break between each series. Once you have read and understand the directions click next.

In Series 1 you will be counting back by 3 from the number given to you by the study coordinator.


Example - 100, 97, 94, 91...


Please confirm what the number you will be counting back from is with the coordinator.


When you're ready, please click "Start". Your voice will start recording and the test will begin in 3 seconds.
